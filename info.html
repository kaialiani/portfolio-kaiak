In January 2022 I moved from a small town in Italy, where life revolved around close-knit communities and a slower pace. This instilled in me a love for detail and a genuine desire to create things that could connect with people. Fueled by this passion, I decided to pursue a career in building digital products. Now, based in the vibrant and design-centric city of Copenhagen, I graduated in Multimedia Design in January 2024 at København Erhvervsakademi, and decided to continue my education starting a Web Development Top-up in 2024.

During my studies, I developed solid skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Next.js. I find particular joy in specializing in bringing functionality and aesthetics together. Figma is my creative playground, where I meticulously design and refine my projects, ensuring an intuitive user experience and a visually stunning outcome. This portfolio serves as a showcase of my recent works, reflecting a fusion of technical skills and design finesse.
