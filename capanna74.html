The client is opening an Italian restaurant in Copenhagen, they have already decided on the name and location, but haven’t designed their visual identity and don’t have a website.

Our goal for the final exam's project, was to develop an easy-to-navigate restaurant website with a simple, clean design, highlighting the restaurant's concept, origin, and affordability. Additionally, we developed a user- friendly online ordering system to provide a takeaway option.

The name of the restaurant is CAPANNA DI SETTANTAQUATTRO, and the location is Gammel Kongevej 74. The main target group is families and students due to the affordable prices. The meaning of the restaurant's name - Capanna - is a small house or hut, and Settantaquattro means 74, which is also the address.
