ABOUT KLIMAKAMPEN

FIGMA

During my three-month internship at København Erhvervsakademi, I had the pleasure of working with the innovative startup Klimakampen to revamp their app's design. As a UX design intern, my goal was to modernize the app's look and feel, making it more engaging and interactive, not just aesthetically pleasing.

The project involved designing new screens for exciting features like a task-swiping system, a streak system, and an overall refresh.
