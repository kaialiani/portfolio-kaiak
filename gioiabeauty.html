Gioia Beauty is an Italian boutique beauty clinic opening in September 2024 that needed a sophisticated online presence to match its high-end services. I was tasked with creating a website that not only showcases the clinic’s offerings but also tells the unique story behind its founding. The goal was to develop a seamless user experience that reflects the brand’s elegance while providing practical functionality for both the client and her customers.

Website design and development

The website serves as a digital hub for Gioia Beauty, combining aesthetics and usability.
It features a visually appealing layout that highlights the clinic's range of services, supported by compelling storytelling that connects visitors with the passion behind the brand.
A booking section was integrated into the site, allowing customers to schedule appointments effortlessly. This booking system is connected to a custom dashboard that enables the client to manage appointments efficiently, providing her with full control over her schedule and client interactions.

Graphics design

In addition to the website, I designed Gioia Beauty's business cards and price list graphics.
